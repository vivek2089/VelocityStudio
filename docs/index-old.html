Frequently Asked Questions

Does Meditime work without internet?
Yes! Meditime uses local notifications that work even when you don't have an internet connection. This ensures you never miss a reminder.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All your medication data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
Can I set different schedules for different medications?
Yes, you can customize the schedule for each medication individually. Set different times, frequencies, and dosages for each of your medications.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Introduction

Velocity Studio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile applications under the domain velocitystudio.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Apps.

This Privacy Policy applies to all current and future apps we release. Please note that not every app collects the same information — some work fully offline, while others may include optional online features.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app and the features you use, we may collect:

  • Information You Provide: Account details (e.g., name, email) if you create an account; health or personal entries (like medication schedules) — stored locally unless you enable online sync or backup; caregiver or emergency contacts (if provided).
  • Automatically Collected Information: Device type, operating system, usage statistics, crash reports, and analytics data. Location data only if you grant permission.
  • Third-Party Services: Our apps may use services such as Firebase (Crashlytics, Analytics, Cloud Messaging), Google Play Services, Apple App Store, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers. These services have their own privacy policies.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Send notifications, reminders, or alerts
  • Analyze crashes and performance
  • Support optional features such as cloud sync or caregiver sharing
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only when:

  • You give explicit consent (e.g., sharing with caregivers)
  • We use service providers who help operate our apps (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Required by law or to protect safety
  • Business transfers (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets)

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. For offline apps, all data remains on your device, and its security depends on your device settings.

Your Choices

  • Manage app permissions such as notifications, location, or contacts through device settings
  • Access, update, or delete information stored in the app
  • Request data deletion (if an account exists) by contacting us
  • Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

International Users

Your information may be processed outside your country. We ensure adequate protection in compliance with applicable laws.

Legal Compliance

We comply with privacy laws such as GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA. Depending on your region, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of our apps after changes means you accept the revised policy.
