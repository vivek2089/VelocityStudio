Frequently Asked Questions

Does Meditime work without internet?
Yes! Meditime uses local notifications that work even when you don't have an internet connection. This ensures you never miss a reminder.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All your medication data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
Can I set different schedules for different medications?
Yes, you can customize the schedule for each medication individually. Set different times, frequencies, and dosages for each of your medications.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2023

Introduction

At Velocity Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed to respect your privacy. Specifically for Meditime:

  • Meditime stores your medication schedule and reminder settings locally on your device
  • We do not collect any personal health information
  • We do not require any account registration
  • We do not track your usage through analytics platforms

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no usage of your information beyond the local functionality of the app:

  • Meditime uses your device's local notification system to remind you to take medications
  • All data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties
  • You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the application

Third-Party Services

Our applications may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for Android apps) - For app distribution and updates
  • Apple App Store (for iOS apps) - For app distribution and updates
  • Notification services - Platform-specific notification systems to deliver reminders

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure the security of your data within our applications. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security also depends on your device's security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
